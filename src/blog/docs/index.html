Welcome to Maian Weblog

Maian Weblog is a FREE, simple, yet powerful PHP journal / blog platform for personal or business use.

Fully HTML5 responsive, it complies with modern standards and offers a powerful feature set for you to post your thoughts and ideas online. For security, it does not support insecure third party plugins like Wordpress.

This software was written with ease of use in mind and contains many great features. It`s a powerful journal / blog system written with some of the latest technology and all presented in a cosmetically appealing interface to provide your business with a stable, reliable journal / blog environment.

Maian Weblog is self hosted and enables you to post your ideas online without worrying about third party subscriptions. A one off payment unlocks the full feature set, but you can use the free version as long as you like. Hope you like it.

Maian Weblog is NOT encoded and should run on any web server out of the box with mcrypt (PHP5.5 to PHP7.0*) or openssl (PHP7.1 or higher) support enabled.

Maian Weblog is NOT open source, licence information can be found on the website.
